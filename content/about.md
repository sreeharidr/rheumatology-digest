## About Rheumatology Digest

Rheumatology Digest is an independent educational project by **Dr. Sree Hari Reddy MD**, consultant rheumatologist (Kurnool, India). Each entry is a concise, original summary of a published paper, guideline or case — written to help clinicians keep up with the rheumatology literature. The site is **non-commercial** and carries no advertising or sponsorship.

## Content & copyright

- **The summaries are original works.** Each post is an independent restatement, in our own words, of the **facts and findings** of the cited source — scientific results, data and clinical recommendations are facts and are not subject to copyright. We do **not** reproduce the original articles' text.
- **We do not reproduce figures, images, tables, photographs or radiological images** from the source articles. All infographics and visuals on this site are **created by us** to illustrate the underlying facts.
- **Every post links to its original source** (authors, journal and DOI) so readers can consult the primary article. Copyright in each original work remains entirely with its authors and publishers.
- If you are an author or publisher and believe any content here oversteps fair use of your work, please **contact us** (see below) and we will promptly amend or remove it.
